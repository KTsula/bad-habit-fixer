Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

The short version: Bad Habit Fixer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your camera feed is processed entirely on your device and never leaves it. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no servers.

Who we are

Bad Habit Fixer is an open-source project created by Keti Sulamanidze and available on GitHub. It is provided free of charge with no commercial intent.

What data we do NOT collect

  • No video, images, or camera frames are recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • No personal information (name, email, location, IP address)
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No user accounts or sign-ups

How the camera is used

The app requests access to your webcam to detect hand and face landmarks in real time using Google MediaPipe. Camera frames are processed in memory on your device only and are immediately discarded after each frame. No video is ever recorded, saved to disk, or sent to any server.

Local storage

Your preferences (selected habit, alert sound choice) are saved in your browser's localStorage. This data is stored only on your device, is never transmitted, and can be cleared at any time through your browser or system settings.

Third-party services

Desktop app (Tauri): All MediaPipe model files and WASM runtime are bundled inside the application. The desktop app makes no network requests during normal operation.

Browser version: MediaPipe WASM files are loaded from cdn.jsdelivr.net and model files from storage.googleapis.com. These are standard CDN downloads of open-source library files. No user data is sent to these services — only the library code is fetched.

Camera permissions

Camera access is controlled by your operating system or browser. The app cannot access your camera without your explicit permission, and you can revoke it at any time through your device settings. The app does not request any other system permissions.

Open source

The complete source code is publicly available at github.com/KTsula/bad-habit-fixer. You are welcome to audit the code to verify that no data is collected or transmitted.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data from any user, including children. Since no personal information is gathered, there are no special provisions necessary for users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR).

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Since the app collects no data, changes are unlikely, but transparency matters.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out by opening an issue on GitHub.
