100% local. Your camera feed is processed on your machine using MediaPipe. No server, no account, no data ever leaves your device.

The browser version works too, but detection pauses when the tab loses focus. The desktop app keeps monitoring in the background.

How it works

1

Open the app

Pick which habit you want to break — eyebrow plucking, nose touching, or hair pulling.

2

Grant camera

The app uses your webcam to track hand and face landmarks in real time. Nothing is recorded.

3

Get alerted

When your hand drifts to the danger zone, you hear a sound. Catches stack up so you can track progress.

Detectable habits

🧠

👃
